Quick Tips

DAILY SECURITY HABITS

Simple habits that keep hackers out. Start doing these today — they take under 10 minutes total.

01
Use 12+ Character Passwords

Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols. Use a password manager like Bitwarden so you never have to remember them.

02
Enable 2FA Everywhere

Two-factor authentication blocks 99.9% of automated attacks. Enable it on every account — email, bank, social media.

03
Never Click Unknown Links

Hover over links before clicking. 90% of hacks start with phishing. If it looks suspicious — it probably is.

04
Keep Software Updated

Unpatched software is an open door for hackers. Enable auto-updates on your OS, browser and all apps.

05
Use a VPN on Public WiFi

On public WiFi, hackers can see everything you type. ProtonVPN's free plan is all you need to stay protected.

06
Back Up Your Data Weekly

Ransomware encrypts your files and demands payment. A weekly backup means you can recover without paying a cent.
